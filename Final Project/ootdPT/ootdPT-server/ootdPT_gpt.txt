1. Waterproof Jacket: Look for a lightweight coat with a hood and plenty of pockets to keep your belongings dry while still protecting you from the rain.

2. Rain Boots: Choose a pair of rain boots that are waterproof and provide enough traction for slippery surfaces.

3. Waterproof Backpack: Consider carrying a waterproof backpack or bag to keep any electronics or important items safe during your rainy day activities. 

4. Rain Pants: Pack a pair of waterproof rain pants to keep your legs dry and comfortable in the rain.

5. Rain Hat: A wide brimmed hat with a sturdy brim can protect your face from the intense rain.

6. scarf: Wear a light scarf around your neck so you can still show off your sense of style while staying protected from the elements.
